Kornelije Stanković (1831-1865), Klavirske varijacije na temu narodne pesme (Piano variations of folk song). Stanković was the first Serbian Romanticist composer (along with composing, he was also engaged in recording the folk and church songs). Varijacije represents a typical example of the chamber music, performed in town homes of the affluent contemporaries of Kornelije Stanković.
