Kornelije Stanković (1831-1865), Klavirske varijacuje na temu pesme 'Što se bore misli moje' (Piano variations of the song 'Što se bore misli moje'). Stanković, the first Serbian Romanticist composer (and melograph) composed these maestral variations of the popular Serbian town song, typical for the chamber music. It is assumed that Prince Mihailo himself was the author of the song 'Što se bore...'
