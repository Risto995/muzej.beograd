Representation of Defterdarova džamija (Defterdar's mosque) at the corner of Obilićev venac and Vuka Karadžića St. (at the present location of restaurant "Proleće"). The mosque was constructed in XVII century, and demolished immediately after 1878, when Serbia won its independence at the Congress of Berlin. A part of the roof and first story of the still existing house are seen to the left of the mosque, and belfry in the bottom of Vuka Karadžića St. belongs to the Lutheran Church..
