Representation of Defterdarova d`amija (Defterdar's mosque) at the corner of Obili}ev venac and Vuka Karad`i}a St. (at the present location of restaurant "Prole}e"). The mosque was constructed in XVII century, and demolished immediately after 1878, when Serbia won its independence at the Congress of Berlin. A part of the roof and first story of the still existing house are seen to the left of the mosque, and belfry in the bottom of Vuka Karad`i}a St. belongs to the Lutheran Church..
