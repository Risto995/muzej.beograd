VIEW OF BELGRADE IN THE PERIOD OF AUSTRIAN GOVERNMENT IN 1717-1739

Panorama represents Belgrade viewed across the mouth of Sava. The upper town, inner fortification, Capuchin monastery and Alexander's barrack are visible and also described textually. A shipyard is depicted on the left bank of Danube (probably the military one "Keiserl. Schiffamt.", pontoon bridges are constructed across the rivers, and the raised redoubts stand on the mouth of Sava, between the river and marshlands (Morast), as well as on the bridgehead on the Banatian side of Danube .

The title at the top of the picture is The town of Belgrade in Serbia, in German language
