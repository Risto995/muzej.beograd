BODENEHR'S ALBUM (FIG. 3)
PLAN OF VIENNA AND ITS SURROUNDINGS

Vienna and its adjacent environs with new ramparts ("Linien"). Entirely inconclusive plan of Vienna. In the lower left corner is a scale in Viennese ells ( klafter ), but lacking numerical markings. Outside of the plan stands one column to the left and two columns to the right containing historical notes and explications. Explanations consist of 136 letter and numerical entries in total. Above the right corner of the plan, just along the border of the print, is the number 192.
