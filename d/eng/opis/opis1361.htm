A view of Old konak (lodge), the house of Stojan Simić constructed in 1842, by the unknown architect. It subsequently became the palace of Karađorđević and thereafter of the Obrenović dynasty. It was placed between the current objects of Old and New Palace. After assassination of the royal couple Draga and Aleksandar Obrenović, it was demolished in 1903. The building consisted of groundfloor and one story and its side facing the street was richly decorated.
