PANORAMA OF BELGRADE IN XVII CENTURY

Belgrade seen from the Danube is represented in this panorama. The Lower town is in the foreground along with harbour and four ships in front of it. Reconstructions of the Upper town are clearly visible, certain turrets were turned into mosques. A settlement outside town walls is noticeable behind the harbour.

In the flag at the top of the picture: "Belgrad oder Griegischweissenburg" - i.e., "Belgrade" written two times, in Latin and German language.
