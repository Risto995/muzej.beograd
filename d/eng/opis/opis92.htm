ALBUM OF CUNO VON QUITZOW
BELGRADE FORTRESS

Representation of Belgrade fortress viewed from the left bank of Sava, precisely at its confluence with Danube . Apart from buildings in the Upper town, the entirely preserved buildings in the Lower town of Belgrade fortress are visible. Two steam-boats, several boats and a raft are represented on the river. It must be noted that it represents the earliest representation of steam-boats on the rivers around Belgrade. Signature C. v. Quitzow 1856 is in the lower left corner.
