ALBUM OF THE TREATY OF POŽAREVAC (FIG. 3)

A sergeant (Sergent), sergeant major bearing a flag, and commander of grenadirs are represented in the rear, all of them following a group of 10 trumpet players (Hautboisten). In the center of the scene there are 8 cavalry page boys behind 6 infantry page boys with an equerry leading them. An emissary rides on the far right - general Feldseigmeister count Virmont surrounded by 12 servants.
