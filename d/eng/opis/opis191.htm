PORTFOLIO OF PICTURES OF THE GREAT ĐORĐE PETROVIĆ KARAĐORĐE (FIG. 6)
PORTRAIT OF MILOŠ OBRENOVIĆ

Representation of Miloš Obrenović in formal clothes wearing turban, two yataghans in silah , and barely perceptible saber hilt next to his left thigh Seals are placed at both sides at the bottom of picture. The left seal is Miloš 's seal in his capacity of Obor-knez (ruling prince) and the right one is placed in his capacity of hereditary prince - princely calpac above the ermine fur (cloak) and armor depicted with cross and four strikers.

Legend: Miloš Tešić Mihailović called M. Obrenović, Karađorđe 's voivode of Rujan and Rudnik, and the second leader (after Loma ) of III (in fact II) Serbian Insurrection), from 12 (25) April 1815, the later Prince of Serbia.
