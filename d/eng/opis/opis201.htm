PORTFOLIO OF PICTURES OF THE GREAT ĐORĐE PETROVIĆ KARAĐORĐE (FIG. 16)
PORTRAIT OF JEVREM NENADOVIĆ

Portrait of the voivode of Tamnava is represented. Formally clad, armed with two pistols ( kubura ) and a saber. An emblem is placed below the portrait - a circle with symbol of the Holy Trinity at the top, warriors and learned men in the middle, between them is a heart with initials E JA N, i.e., (J)Evrem Jakova Nenadovića , as well as the crescent at the bottom. The maxim "With it (the Holy Trinity) I win out (the crescent - islam)".

Legend: Jevrem Jakova Nenadović, Karađorđe 's voivode of Tamnava. Regarding the additional emblem, it may not be excluded that the author of this album, autor_13 was Jevrem's direct descendant.
