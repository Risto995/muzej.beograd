ALBUM OF THE TREATY OF POŽAREVAC (FIG. 15)

Eight horses intended to be offered as gift. Each horse is led by a stableboy. Behind them are loaded camels and mules. The emissary's sleeping wagon then follows. Loaded carts pulled by oxen are in the end of the convoy
