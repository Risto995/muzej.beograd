PANORAMA OF BELGRADE IN XVII CENTURY (WITH FOUR SHARP ANGLES AT THE TOP)

Panoramic view of Belgrade as seen from the left bank of Danube (noted by text). Harbour and the lower town are in front and the suburb outside the walls is to the east. Some earlier panorama of Belgrade was probably used as a model. "Griechisch Weissenburh" is written in the flag on the middle of the sheet.

Author: Nyport, Justus van den (1625 - Utrecht 1692)

Period: 1686

Place: Vienna
