ALBUM OF THE TREATY OF POŽAREVAC (FIG. 14)

In the front is the majordomo of the pasha's Court with a suite of 14 cavalrymen, followed by the musical squadron with musicians and their headmaster. The musicians are arranged in three rows with seven cavalrymen in each of them. In the first row are trumpet players, in the second are drummers and in the third are tambourine players. In the rear are 8 horses intended as gift for Imperial (German - Austrian) emissaries. Each horse is led by a stableboy.
