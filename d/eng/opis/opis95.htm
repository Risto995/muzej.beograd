BATTLE SCENE FROM THE SIEGE OF BELGRADE

Representation of the tremendous defeat of the main Turkish army, overpowered by Imperial forces on 16 August 1717 near Belgrade (in German). It shows decisive moments of the battle between forces of Eugene of Savoy and Turkish army which came to aid the besieged Belgrade. A mighty attack of Imperial army and its intrusion into Turkish camp is in the front. Panorama of Belgrade burning after the bombardment coming from the left bank of Sava is in the rear. Both bridges are represented, a fortified camp below Belgrade, Turkish camp (P), and a very clear view of the location of Turkish artillery (I) later to be occupied by Bavarian grenadirs .

Flag-like label is placed above the scene, on the right side of the picture. Explanations with 15 entries (A - P), is placed below, outside the picture.

Period: August 1717 (published in 1721).
