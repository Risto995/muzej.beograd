SPAAR'S ALBUM - "NOVA ISKRA" COPY (FIG. 4)
A VIEW OF BELGRADE (FORTRESS AND TOWN) FROM THE PRESENT SO-CALLED ZAPADNI VRAČAR

Panorama of Belgrade seen from the lands at the southwest. The Kalemegdan- Terazije ridge is marked out with new constructions built by the Austrians. In the foreground to the right are two large buildings, one of them obviously Alexander's barracks.

Author: Spaar Nicolade, (XVIII century)

Period: 1736

Place: Belgrade
