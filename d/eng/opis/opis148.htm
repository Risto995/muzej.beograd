A VIEW OF THE TOWN OF BELGRADE IN SERBIA

A panorama of Belgrade viewed from Danube . Central parts of the lower town are in the foreground, with harbour and several boats on the river. There is no legend in the engraving, but its accuracy corresponds to the noted period.
