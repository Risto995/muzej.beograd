VIEW OF BELGRADE IN THE MID XIX CENTURY

Panorama represents Belgrade viewed across the Danube . An Austrian border guard (at the border crossing) and three groups of people clad mainly in oriental clothes are in the foreground. In the background, across Danube , is a silhouette of Belgrade with clearly noted arsenals and barracks in the Lower town. A large number of boats and freighters float along both banks of the river.
