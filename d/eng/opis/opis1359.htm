Representation of the Konak (lodge) of Princess Ljubica in Kneza Sime Markovića St., no. 8. Well known master builder Hadži Nikola Živković constructed it after the order of Knez Miloš . Permanent exhibition of the City Museum "XIX century Belgrade ambients" is placed there nowadays. It is one-storied house in the Serbian -Balkan architectural style, placed in the corner and surrounded by a large tract of land. A large, today non-existent, arched gateway built in masonry, is represented in the picture.
