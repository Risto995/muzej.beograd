SPAAR'S ALBUM - "NOVA ISKRA" COPY (FIG. 3)
A VIEW TO THE UPPER TOWN, THE LOWER TOWN, THE SAVA GATE, THE SERBIAN CHURCH AND THE METROPOLITAN BUILDING

Panorama of Belgrade seen from the left bank of Sava. In the foreground, on the right side, stands a pontoon bridge across Sava with the (Serbian) church and the building of Metropolitan palace above it. The fort marked by Sahat kula (Clock tower), the Lower town complex with Nebojša tower covered by pointed (Gothic) roof placed right on the bank of Danube , are on the right side.
