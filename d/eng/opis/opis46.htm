BODENEHR'S ALBUM (FIG. 26)
PLAN OF STARA BANATSKA PALANKA (VIPALANCKA)

Plan of the settlement and fortification of Stara Banatska palanka located on the left bank of Danube , close to Ram. A short description on the left side of the plan notes that this place was liberated on 16 November 1716 under the command of general Mersi. The fort itself, as well as a settlement on the island, is quadrangular with semi-circular towers at each corner, in addition protected by water-ditches. The settlement is fortified by palisade, and linked to the land by pontoon bridge built by Austrians, marked by D). Downstream of it is an old Turkish bridge abandoned for further use. On the left side of the plan is an explanation with A - D entries. Number 96 is marked in the upper right corner.
