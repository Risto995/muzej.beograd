A view of the narrow valley which sharply transforms into high mountainous masses with remainings of two objects on their ridge. A large semi-interred building in the shape of Cyrillic "G" is in the valley to the right. There is a watercourse on the borderline between the valley and rocky masses.

Note on the back: " Ruins of Turkish fortifications on Timok near Nišovac."

Author: Groman I. V.

Period: between 1876 and 1878

Place: The surroundings of Nišovac

Object: photograph

Tehnique: black-and-white, toned

Material: paper, cardboard
