ALBUM OF THE TREATY OF PO@AREVAC (FIG. 4)

The first group on the left consists of 24 emissary's lackeys clad in gray jackets, 15 cavalrymen in triple row in front of them are signified as gentlemen (Cavalliers), also, in the front row there is a sergeant major bearing imperial flag. In front of the noted ones are 16 infantry attendants of the gentry.
