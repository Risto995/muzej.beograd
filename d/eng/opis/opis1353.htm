Representation of Narodni sovjet (People's Council) in Narodnog fronta St. ( at the position of today's number 37). It was built in 1836, and demolished between the two World wars because of the construction of the lyceum for girls (Druga ženska gimnazija). It is the groundfloor house in neoclassicist style with courtyard entrance.
