Panorama of snow covered landscape. Small trees is represented in the foreground and in the back. A group of men clad in traditional costume of rough felt stand in the center, four of them with heads wrapped by scarves (as the Albanians). Behind them is a shack constructed of wooden planks - talpara.

Note on the back: "A view of Krevet near Vitkovac".

Author: Groman I. V.

Period: between 1876 and 1878

Place: Krevet near Vitkovac
