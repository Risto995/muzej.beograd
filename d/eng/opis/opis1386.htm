Hospital shack at Vračar. A large object built in haste. It contains a porch and several people, two of them being hospital staff, are below it. The shack stands below tall trees, and a fence is in the background. According to the evidence of Military museum it should represent "a shack with surgery" - a hardly probable assumption.

Author: Groman I. V.

Period: between 1876 and 1878

Place: Belgrade

Object: photograph - copy

Tehnique: black-and-white

Material: paper

Size: 24 x 18 cm

Entries: hospital

Subentries: field hospital at Vračar.
