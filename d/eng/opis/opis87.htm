ALBUM OF CUNO VON QUITZOW
TOWN CHURCH AT BELGRADE

Following the order of prince Miloš , Saborna (town) church at Belgrade was designed already in 1836. The construction lasted until 1841, and furnishing of its interior lasted for another four years. The church was designed by Franz Janke who stayed in Belgrade between 1834 and 1843.

Old Metropolitan palace is seen in the picture. It was demolished between two World Wars, and the new building of Patriarchy was erected afterwards. A public fountain is placed in front of the church' side facing Kralja Petra st. Signature C v Quitzow 1856 is in the lower right corner.
