Facade of the large storied house with centrally placed entrance. A vacant ground covered by snow is in front of it, and to the left, below the tree, stands a wooden cross enclosed by loose sticks. Military museum possesses a photo of the same building, with a difference that a hospital placed in it was visible. The building represents the Main office (of district) of Jagodina.

Note on the back: "Main office at Jagodina".
