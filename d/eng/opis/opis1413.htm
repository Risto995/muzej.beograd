Representation of the idyllic landscape - left of the large tree, a group of fifteen children sit in the grass around man in civilian suit with tie around collar. Behind them is opened wooden pavilion, with groundplan in the shape of Cyrillic "G", its shorter section closed. In the background stands extremely white house with barred window. Two massive oblique beams are in the far right, probably a part of wooden construction of the church belfry.

" Church dining room, District of Smederevo , no. 2"
