THE CONQUEST OF BELGRADE ON 7 OCTOBER 1789

Representation of victory and conquest of the town and main fortress in Serbia - Belgrade. Depiction of "Glorious conquest of Belgrade" as it is noted in the legend. Tents of the main headquarters are in the center (3), and to the right stands Fieldmarshall Laudon (2) and members of his headquarters. On the left side of the scene rides an adjutant (4) watching the captured Turks passing by (5). The old circumvallation line (6) from 1717 is in the rear, along with new line of defense (7) constructed during the siege of Belgrade in 1789 , mosque (23) and Serbian suburbs behind it (4). Burning Belgrade under artillery fire is in the background. The scene is viewed from the besiege camp at Dedinje (Dedinaberg). A short legend in German language with 1 - 33 entries is placed below the scene.
