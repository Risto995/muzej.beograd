PORTFOLIO OF PICTURES OF THE GREAT \OR\E PETROVI] KARA\OR\E (FIG. 24)
VIEW OF KARA\OR\E'S TOWN AT TOPOLA

A view of Topola and Kara|or|e 's fortified town is represented. Below the picture is a legend: A) town courtyard, B) church courtyard, I, II, and III are town towers, IV church tower; a) Kara|or|e 's court, b) guest lodges (konaci), v) old house - residence of young men, g) well, d) school. Outside of the town are: 1. guesthouse, 2. barrack, 3. stable, 4. nuriska (?) house (nuri in Turkish means bright, light), 5. fountain, 6. Turkish graves, 7. district house, 8. market-place shops, 9. priest's house and 10. municipal house.
