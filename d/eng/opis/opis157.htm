REPRESENTATION OF THE FIGHT BETWEEN AUSTRIAN AND TURKISH BATTLE YAWLS ON DANUBE ON 15 OCTOBER 1788

Representation of the event which took place in the first year of the siege of Belgrade, when the Austrian naval forces under the command of von Magdeburg rejected an attempt of the attack of Turkish yawls on military camp in Zemun , on 15 October 1788. The Turks have lost 6 yawls in the conflict and were forced to retreat. A small and very inaccurate panorama of Belgrade is placed in the upper right corner. Depicted event is described in German language.
