BODENEHR'S ALBUM (FIG. 15)
PANORAMA OF KOŠICE (CASCHAU)

Panorama of Košice with several important settlements around town. Košice itself is thoroughly fortified with two rows of ramparts and a ditch filled with water in front of them. In a short description and history placed in the right bottom, it is noted that Košice was one of the finest towns in the upper Ungaria (actually Slovakia), and that it was overtaken by Imperial forces in 1685.

Above, on the right is the number 30
