ALBUM OF THE TREATY OF POŽAREVAC (FIG. 11)

Five horses covered by silver shields and lustrous dressings led by two grooms. Behind them is an assistant of the main equerry. They are followed by 15 aghas (wearing small beards), 2 interpreters and finally by "trpezni" (table) basha, responsible for serving at the table.

Author: Weiss Johann K.

Publisher: Wolff Jeremias (1663-1724)

Period: 1720-1724

Place: Vienna
