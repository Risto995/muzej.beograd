ALBUM OF THE TREATY OF POŽAREVAC (FIG. 8)

This is the central sheet. Left of the center is the front, i.e., the most important part of Imperial delegation, and on the right is the official part of Turkish delegation. The sheet contains a thorough explanation placed in both upper corners with A-Z entries (24 entries in total). Three columns arranged in the horizontal center of picture are signified systematically as the Imperial column on the left, the Turkish on the right, and the one between them is central. Central column is surrounded by the following representations: two empty chairs - the seats for emissaries, along with a group consisting of A) general Oduyer, B) Rumelian beylerbey Abdullah Dushum-Zade, C and D) Imperial and Turkish interpreters, E, F, G, H, I and K) are Imperial and Turkish delegates, L, M, N, and P) less significant Imperial delegates, noblemen, chaiduts, lackeys, etc., Q) lieutenant Kornhof with 6 small cannons and a crew in the lower left corner, R, S, T and Z) are Turkish representatives - 8 aghas, types of soldiers, beylerbeys horse with groom and alike.
