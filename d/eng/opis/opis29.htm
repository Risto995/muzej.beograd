BODENEHR'S ALBUM (FIG. 9)
PANORAMA OF TOKAY

In the right front there are armed infantryman and cavalryman. In front of them, across Tisza, lays the panorama of Tokay. The fort is protected by palisades , and the second line of defense consists od low ramparts. To the left, beyond the fortress, a part of civil settlement could be seen. Along the left border of the picture there is a text titled: Tockay. The text relates to a small town in Hungary above all famous for its wines.

In the upper right corner is the number 168.

Author: Bodenehr Gabriel, (1664-1758)

Period: After 1718
