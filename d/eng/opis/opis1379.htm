The atrium of the University at Belgrade is represented in the photo. The courtyard of Kapetan Mišino zdanje ( Kapetan Miša's edifice) is visible as well as a part of the main facade (to the left), along with a facade facing the piazzetta of the new building of the Faculty of Philosophy. There are 18 covered carts bearing the emblem of Red Cross. A jade is seen on the left, and bed sheets are drying between infirmary carts. One of the Russian hospitals was placed there during the war.
