Representation of Delijska fountain at the corner of Knez Mihajlova and Vuka Karadžića St. In 1843, the fountain was installed on the location of old ruined fountain. Delijska fountain was demolished in 1899. Left of the fountain stands the house of Jovan Panđela, constructing after the project of architect Jovan Bugarski in 1872, and it still exists today. To its right stands the former hotel "Rusija", a building constructed in 1870 (the author unknown). Similar to Panđel's house, it has also been rebuilt and renovated, and today it is known as the building of "Rudnap". Knez Mihajlova St. extends into the picture's bottom, in direction of Kalemegdan.
