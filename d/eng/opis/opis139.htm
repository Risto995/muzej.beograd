REPRESENTATION OF THE SIEGE OF BELGRADE VIEWED FROM THE LAND

Representation of the siege of Belgrade in autumn 1789 . Fieldmarshall Gideon Laudon (1) is in the foreground with his officers. Next to him and in the rear are troops engaged in the siege of Belgrade. Belgrade is burning, exposed to the cannon fire. A mosque and a watermill are depicted between general Laudon and the town. Next to the fortress (2) are Srpska varoš - Serbian town (4) on the Sava terrace, and a suburb on Danubian bank Vodena varoš - Water town, Wasserstadt (3). Below the title is a legend in German language with 8 entries. A marking "12" is placed above the upper right corner of the picture.
