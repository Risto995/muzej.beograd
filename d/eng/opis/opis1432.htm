Representation of a large white storied house with four gabled roof, richly finished facade, and high (oriental) chimneys. It is placed at the corner, its frontage additionally decorated. Next to it are gate built in masonry and guardhouse painted in colours of Serbian flag (red, blue, white). In front of the building is a flag-pole, also painted in national colours.

It is hand written on the back: "Konak at Para}in", in fact it was the main residence (wartime) of the prince Milan Obrenovi} at Para}in

Author: Groman I. V.

Period: between 1876 and 1878

Place: Para}in

Object: photograph

Tehnique: black-and-white, toned

Material: paper, cardboard

Size: 39,5 (27) x 33,5 (21,2) cm

Entries: building; konak (lodge)

Subentries: residence of the Prince Milan Obrenovi} (in wartime), Konak (lodge) at Para}in.
