PANORAMA OF BELGRADE WITH A GENRE SCENE

Representation of the appearance of Belgrade fortress, viewed from a spot next to the confluence. A group of five men pull the barge (partly visible in the left part of the picture) in the foreground. Behind them is a part of Veliko ratno ostrvo (Great war island), and further in he rear is the fortress of Belgrade. The panorama of fortress (beginning from the right) encompasses a watchtower, as a part of fortification, Sahat kula (Clock tower) and the entire Lower town with military constructions and Neboj≈°a kula (tower). Apart from river fortifications, the coastal bastions of Belgrade fortress are also represented.
