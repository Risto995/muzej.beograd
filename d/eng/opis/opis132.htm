PASSAGE OF THE ROYAL-IMPERIAL ARMY ACROSS SAVA ON 11 SEPTEMBER 1789

The title in German language is placed below the engraving. Zemun and the watchtower at Gardoš (which was placed at the spot of actual monument of the millennium from 1895) are represented as well as the troops crossing bridges and their arrangement around Belgrade. The Bežanija embankment (D) is noted as a road leading to the bridges. A legend in German language with 14 entries (A - O) is placed below the picture, with a site of Dedinje noted as Deschina Berg. A letter "j" is marked below the right corner of the picture.

Period: 1789

Object: engraving

Tehnique: copper engraving

Material: paper

Size of sheet: 36 x 22,3 cm

Legend: Text is in German language

Entries: battle scene

Subentries: passage of the Royal-Imperial army across Sava in 1789.
