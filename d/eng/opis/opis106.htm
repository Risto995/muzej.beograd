SPAAR'S ALBUM - "NOVA ISKRA" COPY (FIG. 6)
ALEXANDER'S OR WURTEMBERG'S MILITARY BARRACKS

A view of newly accomplished barracks. Several groups of people are represented in the large vacant space in front of it. They are clad mostly in oriental (Turkish) clothes, a few of them are in traditional dressings, and only one among them (to the far right) wears European clothes. Monumental building of Alexander's (Wurtemberg's) barracks is behind them and its main facade is shown. The main entrance with a large square- based terrace above it, is placed in its center. The building has two wings, each with ten windows and the central tract. The building consists of the groundfloor and two stories. The left, lateral facade is visible, representing a modest copy of the main facade, also with the main entrance and a terrace above it.

All the noted facts may imply that the building had a size of 22 x 36 m.
