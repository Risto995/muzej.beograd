A courtyard with house, enclosed by the fence of sticks with tips covered by two gabled planks. These are arranged regularly and reinforced by strong wooden posts which are higher than the fence. At the bottom is courtyard entrance, without doors. A large house with elevated groundfloor is on the right, with two entrances (at the facade an in its rear). Entrances are covered and decorated by foliage.
