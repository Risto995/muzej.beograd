ALBUM OF CUNO VON QUITZOW
A VIEW OF BELGRADE FROM TAŠMAJDAN

The Tašmajdan cemetery is in the front, and Belgrade, Veliko ratno ostrvo (Great War island), and an outline of the left Danube bank are in the rear. Among the objects, the Batali mosque is visible (with its minaret already ruined) and the belfry of Saborna (cathedral) church to its right. Signature C. v. Quitzow 1856 is in the lower left corner.
