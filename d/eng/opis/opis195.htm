PORTFOLIO OF PICTURES OF THE GREAT ĐORĐE PETROVIĆ KARAĐORĐE (FIG. 10)
KARAĐORĐE WINS OUT NUMAN PASHA

Representation of the battle in the moment when entrenched Serbs begin the assault, after having rejected Turkish attack. Karađorđe is in the center of the scene, riding a horse. A text below, both with the title is: Karađorđe wins out Numan pasha of Peć, with 20.000 Albanians, at Sjeničko polje, Suvodol, in the end of may 1809.
