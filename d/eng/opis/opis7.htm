ALBUM OF THE TREATY OF POŽAREVAC (FIG. 5)

High dignitary of the Church is riding behind other 5 clergymen riding horses. In front of the clergymen rides emissary's secretary, accompanied by two infantry servants. They are preceded by 18 noble cavalrymen with a flag in the middle of the first line of a triple row. In front of them is the Court Feldmarshall.
