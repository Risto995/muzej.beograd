BELGRADE, A VIEW FROM DANUBE IN MID XIX CENTURY

The engraving represents the southeastern view of Belgrade. A girl from the environs of Belgrade, in traditional costume with shoulder pole on which loads are hung is depicted in the foreground. Two riders on the road leading to the south are approaching her (one of them covered by cloak could be the mail courier - the Tartar). Belgrade is seen in the background.
