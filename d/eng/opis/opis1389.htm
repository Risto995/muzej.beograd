Representation of the courtyard facade of Old konak (see UR/3759 and UR/3760). The building has only groundfloor on this side due to the sloping grounds. A guardhouse is next to it, and the entrance is further away, toward present Dragoslava Jovanovića St. Part of palatial park is seen to the right.
