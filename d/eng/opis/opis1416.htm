Panorama represents a trunk of an old tree, a row of willows (?) and Morava river to its left, and to the right, is a far hill descending steeply to Morava. Next to the tree is a man in uniform, and a line of trenches behind it. A flag-pole is placed upon the ridge.

Note on the back: "Morava and the last redoubt of Đunis"

Author: Groman I. V.

Period: between 1876 and 1878

Place: Đunis

Object: photograph

Tehnique: black-and-white, toned

Material: paper, cardboard

Size: 39,5 (27) x 33,5 (21,2) cm
