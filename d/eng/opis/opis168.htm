PANORAMA AND BATTLE SCENE OF THE CONQUEST OF BELGRADE IN 1521

Representation of the appearance of Belgrade at the time of Turkish aggression in August 1521 . Belgrade is seen from Veliko ratno ostrvo (Great War island) with its eastern coast visible at the bottom of picture. A cartouche with legend end entries A-I, and "pag.54" above it is placed in the corner. The Upper town of Belgrade fortress is clearly visible with inner fortification - the castle (C), the Lower town from watermill on Sava (D) until eastern suburbs (E) and Turkish camp at the east of Belgrade (F). Retreat of the defenders is seen upon the steep road leading from Lower town (A) to the Upper town. After that, negotiations took place and Belgrade fell into Turkish hands ( Suleiman the Magnificent) on 29 August 1521 . A group of ships, in the left part of the scene, departs with Hungarian and German crew, while the local population and Serbian part of the crew were transported to the environs of Constantinople.

It is written in German: Turkish siege of the fortress and town of Greek Belgrade in 1521 .
