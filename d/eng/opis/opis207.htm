PORTFOLIO OF PICTURES OF THE GREAT \OR\E PETROVI] KARA\OR\E (FIG. 23)
PORTRAIT OF KARA\OR\E PETROVI]

Portrait of Kara|or|e Petrovi} with high decoration and ribbon across the chest. He holds a calpac with tuft of feathers in the right hand. Flags and arms are deposited below the portrait, and in the center, in the wreath of oak leaves stands the armour of Kara|or|e (seal: Crown, oval shield with double headed white eagle below it), and at the bottom are armours of Serbia and Bosnia. Text in the seal is as follows: "By Gods Grace Georgije Petrovi} Ruler of the whole Serbia and Bosnia". On both sides of the seal the flag is inscribed :

..........

Above Kara|or|e are the cherubs, one of them crowned and holding the inscription:

.........

Right at the top stands a crown, and a wreath of armours descends from it to the left: Serbian, Iapodic, Dalmatian, Dardanian, armour of Ram, Herzegowina, Dacia, Illyria, Rascia, Bosnia and Dubrovnik. To the right of the crown the following armours are lined up: Bulgaria, Moesia, Slavonia - on Sava, Slavonia - on Danube , Srem, Tribalia, Macedonia, Tessaly, Greece, Epirus, Albania and finally the Empire of Serbia.

Legend: \or|e Petrovi}, Kara|or|e , Supreme Leader and Ruler of Serbian people.
