Representation of the old Ministry of finances in Admirala Geprata St., between Nemanjina and Kneza Milo{a St.. It was constructed in 1838 and intended for Knez Milo{ ' s palace, but the Ministry of finances, Council of the State and other governmental offices were settled there in instead. It was demolished in the Second World war. A side facing the street with young trees is represented with an armour at its frontage.
