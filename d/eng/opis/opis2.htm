ALBUM OF THE TREATY OF POŽAREVAC (FIG. 1)

Representation of convoy advancing from the left to right. Loaded cart pulled by four horses is described as the luggage cart and it is explained that there were 320 carts of that kind; then follows a loaded cart pulled by four horses - a cart with gifts representing 20 other carts of this kind. On the far right is a luxurious carriage pulled by six richly adorned horses, along with an explanation that there were 30 carriages of that kind.
