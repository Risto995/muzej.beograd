BODENEHR'S ALBUM (FIG. 24)
PLAN OF MEHADIA (MEDIA OR MEADIA)

The plan shows a small fortified town of Mehadia , cca. 10 km away from Orsava. According to town description and history on the left side of the plan, the town and fortification were destroyed by Tatars, but it was subsequently renovated by the cavalrygeneral count Mersi. Explanation on the right side of the plan points out the erection of 15 feet wide breastworks and B) a ditch of the same size reinforced by palisades . Around the town are villages devastated by Tatars. Above the right corner of the plan there is a mark of number 62.
