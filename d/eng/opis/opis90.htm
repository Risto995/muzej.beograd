ALBUM OF CUNO VON QUITZOW
PRINCE'S COUNTRY HOUSE AT TOPČIDER NEAR BELGRADE

The object concerned is the Konak (lodge) of prince Miloš , built in 1834. It was designed by Hadži Nikola Živković (1792-1870), who supervised its building as well. Left to the Konak is Topčiderska church, built in the same year - 1834, but its designer remained unknown. Signature C. v. Quitzow 1856 is in the lower right corner.
