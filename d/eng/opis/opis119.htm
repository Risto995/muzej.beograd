MILITARY PLAN OF THE IMPERIAL AUSTRIAN ARMY ON 15 JUNE 1717

Representation of the Imperial army after successful crossing of the Danube on 15.o6. 1717 . (The title is placed inside of the plan). It is placed within a symmetrical oval medallion with a turban at its top - marking that it is Turkish, flanked by a spear, halberd, and two flags at the sides. Two crossed cannons are placed at the bottom of medallion. Representation of the advancement of military units and organization of crossing of the Danube at Vi{njica is placed in the upper part to the right. A plan of Belgrade with representation of advancement of Austrian army before the establishment of fortified camp is laced in the center of the plan. The lower, separated part of the picture represents the arrangement of troops (Ordre de bataille) in the field camp with names of more important commanders, and the strength the besieging forces is represented below: 61 infantry battalions and 164 cavalry squadrons ( cuirassiers , dragons and hussars ).

Period: 15.06.1717
