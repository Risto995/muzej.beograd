BATTLE SCENE IN AUGUST 1717 WITH PANORAMA OF BELGRADE

Scene of the violent battle on 17 August 1717 between Christian army under the command of Eugenee of Savoy and Turkish army led by serasker Halil-pasha having arrived to help the besieged Belgrade. Violent assault of infantrymen - the grenadirs , and the cavalry rushing into Turkish camp, is represented on the left side. Turkish retreat is depicted on the right side, along with a part of large luxurious Turkish tent (pasha's residence?), and abandoned Turkish cannon stands in the corner. In the center of the scene is the chief commander Eugenee of Savoy himself, riding a horse in armour and head covered by helmet. In the background, up to the bank of Danube are battle representations. A small panorama of Belgrade viewed across Veliko ratno ostrvo (Great War island), titled "Belgrado", stands separated in the upper right corner. It lacks any factual relation to the scene, being just a marking of the place of these events. Below the scene is the text in Italian language: "a battle which took place in August 1717 , on the bank of Sava between Imperial army and Turks, under the command of illustrious Prince Eugenee of Savoy , accomplished by the victory of Imperial army," and then "1426".
