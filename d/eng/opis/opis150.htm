BELGRADE, AS IT IS SEEN FROM ZEMUN

A view of Belgrade seen from the mouth of Sava and Veliko ratno ostrvo (Great War island). Two freighters are floating on Dunavac. The Uppervtown (A), Lower town (a), suburb on the terrace of Danube (b), Serbian town on the terrace of Sava (c), Cigansko (gypsy) settlement (d), remains of the trenches of Eugenee of Savoy (h), Dedinje (e), Avala (f) and Vračar (g). Panorama contains a legend in Hungarian language with 15 entries. Roman number "V" is placed above the upper right corner, probably a numeration of an album or possibly of an atlas.
