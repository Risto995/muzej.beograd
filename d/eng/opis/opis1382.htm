Courtyard of the storied house is represented with two hospital carts bearing flags and emblems of Red Cross. One of the emblems is inscribed "Transport Britanskog narodnog društva" (in Cyrillic letters, i.e., "Transport of the British People Society"). A large group of people stands in the courtyard with a nurse and a wounded man on crutches.

Author: Groman I. V.

Period: between 1876 and 1878
