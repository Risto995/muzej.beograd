PORTFOLIO OF PICTURES OF THE GREAT ĐORĐE PETROVIĆ KARAĐORĐE (FIG: 4)
PORTRAIT OF HAJJI PRODAN GLIGORIJEVIĆ

Representation of standing figure of Hajji Prodan Gligorijević in formal clothing wearing calpac . With his right arm he leans upon another rifle (tančica) and holds the yataghan stuck into silah by his left hand. Other two pistols ( kubura , short firearm) are also stuck into silah .

Legend: Hajji Prodan Gligorijević. Karađorđe 's voivode of Starovlah and leader of II Serbian Insurrection (in fact Hajji Prodan's uprising) from 21 September (4 October) till the end of November 1814.

Publisher: Printing firm "Schimek & Standart"

Period: 1883

Place: Vienna
