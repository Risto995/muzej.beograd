Representation of the hospital pavilion in Topčider. Wooden pavilion at Topčider was transformed into field hospital. It is the same pavilion as in the picture UR/3775, but there are more people in front of the pavilion in this photograph. Left of the entrance stands a nurse, and to the far left is an officer of Serbian army with saber.
