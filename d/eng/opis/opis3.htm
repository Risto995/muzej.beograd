ALBUM OF THE TREATY OF POŽAREVAC (FIG: 2)

Seven loaded mules covered by red velvet dressings and accompanied by two drivers are seen in the front, preceded by The Second grenadier squadron which is followed by its commander. The noted squadron is preceded by The First grenadier squadron with four drummers in the first row.
