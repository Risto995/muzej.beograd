BATTLE OF BELGRADE IN AUGUST 1717

Victorious fight of the Imperial forces under the command of Eugenee of Savoy against the Turks, at Belgrade on 16 August 1717 . The big battle and Christian victory are thoroughly represented in several plans. A scene of the invasion of Turkish camp and count Hauben's death (H) are represented in the foreground, to the right. Prince Eugenee is depicted in the very center leading a cavalry detachment (F). Echelons of (mainly) the cavalry are represented on the left side with their commanders, along with infantry assault on Turkish lines under the command of Herbstein (M). Panorama of Belgrade is depicted in the rear (1) with camp of Christian army below it, between the circumvallation (3) and contravallation (2) line. The background plan of the picture represents the settlements placed along Danube : Vi{njica (4), Slanci (Schaklan) - (5), Grocka and Smederevo . In Banat: Ov~a (Otsa) - (8), Pan~evo (10), Crepaja (9) and Baranda (Pandera) - (11); in Srem: Zemun (12), Karlovi~i} ? (Carloz) - (13), Gradoz ? (14) and Slankamen (16). Onthe Serbian bank along Sava, only Pale` (15) was marked (modern Obrenovac ).

Legend in Dutch language with A - S entries is placed below text, dedicated to the troops and battle itself, as well as a second part with 1 - 21 entries which explain the objects and settlements.
