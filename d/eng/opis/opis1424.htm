A broad panorama of the plains ending by a ridge of low hills in the background. A part of village intersected by trenches is in the foreground - "Morava valley, village of Deligrad trenches at Deligrad", and "Đunis" behind it, and in the background on the left are "Jastrebac and Krevet". To the right of Đunis remarkable for its flag-pole, are "Kaonik", "Gora Sv. Romana" and "Redoubts at Krivi vis" (all notes under quotation are read from the text below the picture).

Note on the back: "The Morava valley between Deligrad and Đunis".
