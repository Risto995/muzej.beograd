BODENEHR'S ALBUM (FIG. 13)
PANORAMA OF TIMISOARA (TEMESWAR)

A large mill on the river bank is placed in frontal part of the panorama of Timisoara. Behind it, a bridge leads to the fortified town, surrounded by canals filled with water. Rectangular fort reinforced by circular tower is placed in part of the town seen on the right. On the right side, outside the town, is a settlement with large Christian house of worship. According to the short description and history placed below the picture, the town was located "between Danube and Transylvanian forests", a surprisingly imprecise description. It is noted that Turks conquered it in 1552, and that Christians took it back after the famous battle of Petrovaradin in October 1716.
