BODENEHR'S ALBUM (FIG. 25)
PLAN OF PAN^EVO

Plan of Pan~evo with description and short history. Its location on Tami{ not far from Belgrade is noted as well as that general Mersi liberated it from the Turks after the conquest of Timisoara on 9 November 1716. Central part of fortification A), the castle, is quadrangular with semi-circular towers at each corner. It is protected by a ditch filled with water. B) The settlement (small town, palanka ) is placed between the fortification and Tami{. Four newly established forts were marked by D) and one among them is located on the right bank of Tami{ and protects a pontoon bridge with an access to the town. A village C) is placed upstream of Pan~evo , outside the fortification. On the right, outside of the plan, is an explanation with A - F entries. Number 138 is marked in the upper left corner of the plan.
