SCHEMATIC PLAN OF THE BELGRADE FORTRESS

This plan was created after renovation and construction of the two-horned bastion - according to the plan of the Venetian Andrea Cornarro. Both two-horned bastions (Hornwerk) are drawn although only the eastern one has been accomplished. The plan lacks precise designations and legends. The Lower town (Donja varoš - Wasser Stadt), and the Belgrade castle are marked in the area of Upper town (text is in German language).

The scale in fathoms (Toises) is marked in the area of Lower town - 100 fathoms are 4,5 cm (cca 1 : 5.000). The author's signature is below the scale with a note that he sojourned temporarily at the "red hillside" (rothen Han - probably Hang, i.e., a hillside). The wind rose is placed in the center of the left side, and the plan is rotated 90 degrees counter-clockwise.
