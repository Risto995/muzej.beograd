BATTLE SCENE OF THE ATTACK ON ŠABAC

Representation of the attack on Šabac, as the beginning of military campaign in the last Austrian-Turkish war. In the foreground are Austrian troops and command, and in the rear is Šabac in blaze and smoke (on the high hill!). The legend is in German language with 13 letter entries (a - n) and 7 numerical entries. The Emperor Joseph II (a), fieldmarshall Lasi (b), general Stab (c) are represented in the foreground to the right, followed by heavy cavalry units - the cuirassiers during attack (h). Number 1 is placed above the upper right corner.
