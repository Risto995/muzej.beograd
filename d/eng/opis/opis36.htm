BODENEHR'S ALBUM (FIG. 16)
PANORAMA OF EPERJEŠ (EPERIES)

Panorama of the fortified Eperješ with mighty ramparts in two levels, reinforced by circular towers. A part of the town fortification is protected by water. According to a description, at the bottom left, the town was located in the vicinity of Polish frontier and had a status of the Imperial open city. It is noted that famous reformist (protestant) lyceum existed there before Turkish subjugation.

In the upper right corner is the number 48
