Representation of the Church of the Holy Assumption (Vaznesenska crkva) in Kneza Miloša St. No. 18, between Narodnog fronta and Admirala Geprata St. Its frontage in Admirala Geprata St. is visible as well as its northern facade looking over desolate ground. A fence (non-existent now) built in masonry encircles the church, and a large tree stands on the right side. According to the evidence obtained by IAB (Istorijski Arhiv Beograda) from Moscow, the photo represents "The Church of Invalides and the mulberry tree under which the delegates (of the St. Andrew assembly taking place nearby in the so-called Great brewery) have excavated a trench."

Author: Groman I. V.

Period: between 1876 and 1878

Place: Belgrade
