Representation of the right bank of Sava not far from its mouth. A ship with number "4" on its bow is anchored next to it. A closed structure with small windows, single-winged door and Red Cross emblem at its side is built upon the boat. British flag stands on the bow of the ship, and Red Cross flag is in the rear of the noted structure. Unrecognizable flag is at the stern (according to navigation rules and territorial waters principle, it should have been the flag of the Principality of Serbia). Both in the front and rear decks are nine people, one among them wearing (British ?) uniform. It probably is the ship for transport of wounded men, possibly containing a surgery.
