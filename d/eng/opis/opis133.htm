PANORAMA OF BELGRADE VIEWED FROM DANUBE

The picture represents a view of Belgrade across the Danube , from Banat, so that a densely settled town is seen on the Danube terrace. Austrian soldiers and officers are depicted next to the cannons, on the left bank of Danube . Positions of Turkish army are noted in the rear of the town. The engraving is coloured in a rough, unskilled way, and the thick layers of red (on town roofs and fortress, and the uniforms of Austrian soldiers) and blue paint (soldier uniforms) have thus literally obscured the picture. Below it, in German language is written: The Imperial-Royal army.
