BATTLE SCENE AND PANORAMA OF BELGRADE

A view of Belgrade fortress, Greek White town (in German). The battle scene on the engraving includes a number of events related to the siege of Belgrade in 1688 . To the left is a representation of the panorama of Belgrade with parts of the burning Lower town and Turkish retreat from water and land (F) , i.e., the final part of siege - conquest of Belgrade on 6 September 1688 . In the right side of the picture are ships bringing food and ammunition to the besiege army. On the left bank of Sava is the camp of besiege army and representation of forcing Sava river across Ada Ciganlija (island), as well as the construction of a bridgehead south of Belgrade.

Consequently, a number of events taking place during more than two months of siege is represented. The title is placed within flaglike label above the panorama. Explanation contains 13 entries (A- N) below the panorama of town, and the author's initials are in the lower right corner.
