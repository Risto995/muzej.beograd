Representation of the Terazije fountain and Terazije , an area between present hotels "Moskva" and "Balkan". The fountain was installed in 1860, and in 1911 it was transferred to Topčider because of the reconstruction of Terazije . In 1980s it was reinstalled at Terazije . Left of the fountain is the storied building at the location of present hotel "Moskva", and to its right is the old hotel "Balkan", which was demolished in mid 1930s because a new hotel had to be erected there
