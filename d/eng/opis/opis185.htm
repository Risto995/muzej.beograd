PORTFOLIO OF PICTURES OF THE GREAT ĐORĐE PETROVIĆ KARAĐORĐE

Frontispiece and back cover are of stiff purple cardboard with gold printed text. Text was effaced in the course of time and thus hardly readable, and some parts of it are absolutely unreadable.

Frontispiece: Title in the form of gateway with an armour at its top. Above it stands an archway with a maxim: "Teć'e voda kud je tekla - srpska majka to je rekla". In the gateway fields are dates of important battles in Serbian insurrections.

Back cover: At the top stands an eagle holding a snake in its beak, above the band with inscription "Topolski". It is an obvious allegory of Karađorđe , the eagle of Topola stifling the Turkish snake. At the bottom is a fountain and a maxim inscribed around it: "Pritisnuta jače to na više skače".

Title: "Portfolio of pictures of the Great Đorđe Petrović Kara - Đorđe, the supreme leader, liberator and ruler of Serbia. Pictures of his voivodes and heroes, collected and lithographed by Konstantin N. Nenadović , the Royal Serbian artillery captain in retirement."

Author: Nenadović Konstantin

Publisher: Printing firm "Schimek & Standart"

Period: 1883

Place: Vienna
