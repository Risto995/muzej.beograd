PORTFOLIO OF PICTURES OF THE GREAT ĐORĐE PETROVIĆ KARAĐORĐE (FIG: 3)
PORTRAIT OF THE PRINCE ALEKSA NENADOVIĆ

The Prince is represented in formal clothing, with pistol ( kubura ) and knife stuck into silah , and fur hat (šubara) in the left hand. Aleksa Stev.(ana) Nenadović , Obor-knez (ruling prince) of Valjevo, slayed by the dahis on 23. January (5 February) 1804 at Valjevo.
