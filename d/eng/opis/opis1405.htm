Panorama of the gloomy landscape under snow. Two rows of destroyed fences are seen on the plateau which turns into elevation after several hundred meters. A tree with low branching is in the foreground.

Note on the back: "Section of Đunis from direction of Sv. Nestor (Sv. Nestor is an elevation)".

Author: Groman I. V.

Period: between 1876 and 1878

Place: Đunis

Object: photograph
