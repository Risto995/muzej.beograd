PORTFOLIO OF PICTURES OF THE GREAT ĐORĐE PETROVIĆ KARAĐORĐE (FIG. 13)
PORTRAIT OF SIMA MILUTINOVIĆ - SARAJLIJA

Portrait of Sima Milutinović in classical romanticized manner with a scroll of paper in left hand, while writing with quill upon a sheet of paper. He leans on the bookshelf behind him. Below the portrait are symbols of his vocations - book, lyre and sword.

Legend: Sima Milutinović - Sarajlija or Čubra Čojković, great Serbian poet and writer, and a combatant in I insurrection in lines of the naked sons.

Author: Nenadović Konstantin

Publisher: Printing firm "Schimek & Standart"

Period: 1883

Place: Vienna

Size of print: 15,2 x 22,5 cm

Size of sheet: 15,2 x 22,5 cm
