ASSASSINATION OF HAJJI-MUSTAFA PASHA

Assassination of the Turkish appointee in Belgrade executed by janissaries on 2.12.1801. is represented in the engraving. The assassination of the vizier of Belgrade took place in his konak (lodge) in the Upper town of the Belgrade fortress. While the assassin (5) shoots at pasha (2), three janissaries - dahis (4) are preventing an agha from the pasha's escort (3) to respond in help. A severe oppression of dahis ensued, and consequently it triggered the First Serbian insurrection two years after the noted event. A legend with five entries is placed below the picture.
