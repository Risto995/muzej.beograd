ALBUM OF THE TREATY OF PO@AREVAC (INTRODUCTORY TEXT)

The covering sheet contains a dedication of the author Johan Conrad Weiss to the Commander of Belgrade general Feld-vacht Meister, the Commander of infantry regiment and the GENERAL IN COMMAND OF THE KINGDOM OF SERBIA Oduyer. The author himself (J. C. Weiss) explains himself that it represents a faithful description of the meeting of Imperial and Otoman high emissaries on 15 June 1719, in the vicinity of Para}in. The meeting was greeted by triple salute of artillery, cavalry and infantry.
