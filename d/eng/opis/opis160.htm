SCENE OF TURKISH SURRENDER AFTER THE SIEGE IN 1789

Historical presentation of events on 8 October 1789 , with defeated Turkish crew of Belgrade putting its arms down to the conqueror. In the center of the scene are Fieldmarshall Laudon (E) and Turkish commander of Belgrade (C) supervising the disarmament of the Belgrade defensive forces (D). Belgrade itself is fragmentarily represented, with perceptible destructions after the long siege, bombardments and assaults. Only the "main" mosque is marked among objects.

A richly decorated rococo cartouche with portrait of the Roman Emperor Carus, stands at the top of picture.

A legend with A - E entries is combined with the title at the top of engraving.
