In the foreground stands a group of about forty people arranged in semicircle between houses (the roof of one house is seen to the left, roof of another house is to the right in background, and in to the right in foreground is the corner of third building built in stone masonry. The first on the left is a priest, while the man and woman standing in the center are probably from Negotinska Krajina, according to their clothes. Hence it probably is representation of the surroundings of Knja`evac or Zaje~ar. Hills are seen the background.
