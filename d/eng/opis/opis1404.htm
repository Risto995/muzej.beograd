Panorama of the town in the valley between hills. Several large houses, and, to the left, rather large church with wooden belfry somewhat far of it. Leafless tree is in the foreground with ten people, men and children clad in rough felt dressings. Starting from the tree, along the street, are lined up posts with stretched wires (two wires). The wires are also stretched across the tree (telephone line?).

Note on the back : "Ražanj, from direction of Deligrad."
