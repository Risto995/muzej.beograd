Representation of village countryside. A peasant is in the foreground, head covered by scarf, clad in dressing of rough felt. A pistol ( kubura ) handle (or hilt of the knife - yataghan?) protrudes from his silah . A long pipe (čibuk) is in his right hand. Behind him is a well with sweep (đeram), and a young man taking water from it. Destroyed fences are in the background, along with a hut covered by hay (reeds?), and a sty.

Note on the back: "Village well, district of Smederevo ". An identical photograph in Military museum is titled. "Type of peasant from the surroundings of Belgrade, 1877".
