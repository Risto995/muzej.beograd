Hospital of the Red Cross Society is represented, a groundfloor house with central entrance and a few people in front of it. Two boards with Red Cross emblems are placed upon it. On the right boar one may read the inscription in Cyrillic letters: "Engleska (?) bolnica" (English (?) hospital"). Three flags are placed on the house, and according to its roof it may be assumed that the building had groundplan in the shape of Cyrillic "P", its courtyards tracts being higher than the part facing the street. Position of flags and height of courtyard tracts may imply that it is a different view of the house represented in photograph UR/10371, shot in 180 degrees angle. Sloping grounds indicate that the building was placed in Karađorđeva St.
