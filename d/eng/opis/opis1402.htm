Three cannons are represented in the foreground with guns half turned to the right. Two huts are standing behind, the left one being higher and larger. The photographer stood on somewhat lower altitude than the surrounding terrain. Thin trees are seen in the far left.

Note on the back: "Hospital barracks at Ćuprija on Morava" is probably correct, in contrast to the note on identical specimen from Military museum stating that these are "Cannons in the Upper town (of Belgrade fortress) in 1875/76", and that is highly improbable.
