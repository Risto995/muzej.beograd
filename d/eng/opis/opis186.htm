PORTFOLIO OF PICTURES OF THE GREAT ĐORĐE PETROVIĆ KARAĐORĐE (FIG. 1)
INSURRECTION AT ORAŠAC

Representation of the crowd of people with raised yataghans. Karađorđe is in the foreground with Serbian tricolour with a cross, and a priest with raised cross stands next to him.

Legend: Tanasije Antonijević, the priest of Bukovina serves the church ritual 1. Karađorđe hands the banner over to Tanasko Rajić 2. Obor-knez (ruling prince) Marko 3. Stanoje Glavaš 4. Vule Ilić 5. Hajduk Veljko 6. Vasa Čarapić 7. Janko Katić 8. Knez (prince) Sima Marković 9. Arsenije Loma 10. Jovan Jakovljević and many other leading heroes. Title: "Insurrection of Karađorđe against the Turks, at Orašac on 2 February 1804 (according to new calendar on 15 February), on day of the Holy Visitation of the Virgin. Gathering of the leading heroes and insurrectionist against the Turks. The election of Kara- Đorđe for the Supreme Leader and Ruler of Serbia. Oath and Chrism."

Author: Nenadović Konstantin

Publisher: Printing firm "Schimek & Standart"
