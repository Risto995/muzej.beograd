BODENEHR'S ALBUM (FIG. 11)
PANORAMA OF GYULA (GIULA)

Panorama of Gyula, the town and the fortress. The town is fortified by ramparts and a ditch filled with water. Aside from town, the suburbia protected by palisades could also be seen. In the lower left corner is a note that Giula was not far from Veliki Varadin (Oradea), that its importance was small, but that its fortress, taken by Christians in 1693, has however been preserved.

Up on the right is the number 71
