Representation of hospital shack in the environs of Belgrade. Improvised, half opened shack (reminding to a barn), under tall trees. It is approached by he path made of planks. A large group of people stands in front of it, wounded men, and nurses (one of them female). The man at the far right serves the guests holdings a tray in his left arm.
