Representation of the building of Učiteljska škola (School for Teachers) at the corner of Narodnog fronta and Dobrinjska St., constructed around 1860, after the project of architect Andrija Vuković. It was preserved until nowadays. Originally it was not intended for a school. Its side facing Narodnog fronta St. and Church of the Holy Assumption
