GENRE SCENE AND VIEW OF BELGRADE IN 1791

A very small panorama (format of the picture is only 13,5 x 6,5 cm) with double engraved rim, represents three Austrian soldiers - hussars , two of them riding, and Belgrade in the background viewed across the Great war island (Veliko ratno ostrvo). The viewpoint is similar to that on panoramas made during the siege of Belgrade 1788- 1789 .

Format, content and quality of the paper may imply that the sheet was torn off from some English album of smaller format. Below the picture is written in English language: A view of Belgrade. Signature of the draftsman is at the bottom left, and of the engraver to the right, and at the bottom of the imprint (partially erased and unreadable): "Published by Dr. Truster Aug.(ust) 1. 1791."
