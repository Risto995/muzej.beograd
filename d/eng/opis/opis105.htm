SPAAR'S ALBUM - "NOVA ISKRA" COPY (FIG. 5)
GENERAL VIEW OF BELGRADE (FORTRESS AND TOWN) FROM A LOCATION ABOVE TAŠMAJDAN

Panorama of Belgrade with suburbia in the foreground. Karlstadt - the German part of the town is on the right, and Serbian settlement is on the left, probably at the intersection of modern Balkanska St. and Narodnog fronta St. - i.e., Nova srpska savska varoš (New Serbian town on Sava) beyond the bastion line of the town fortification. Intramural Belgrade with Serbian church on the bank of Sava along with Metropolis, the Upper town with large buildings and new edifices on the line between the Fort and (modern) Terazije , are represented in the rear of panorama. A church tower with several mosques is seen to the far right.
