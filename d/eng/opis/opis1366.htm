Representation of the Lutheran Church in Vuka Karadžića St. No. 7, constructed in 1860 after the project of architect Franz Zelony, demolished in 1937. A fence encircling the church courtyard is seen to the right, and a worn-out wooden fence encircling a vacant tract of land (present location of the building of Serbian Academy of Science s and Arts) stands on the left.
