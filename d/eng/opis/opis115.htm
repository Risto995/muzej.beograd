PLAN OF THE SIEGE OF ŠABAC

The map describes a seizure of the fortress of Šabac on 24 April 1788. Apart from the plan of siege and seizure of Šabac, the settlements of Cigansko predgrađe and Predgrađe Bair (Bejel) - an area around the modern railway station - are represented as destroyed by fire. A medallion with portrait of the prince Charles de Ligne is placed in the upper left corner, decorated by wreath, flags and cannons. Below the map (plan) is a legend in German language which enumerates the names of commanders: colonel Ant(al) Esterhazy, E.H. ( Erzherzog ) Ferdinand, Turn, Nick(ola) Esterhazy, Caprara, Palfy, Nadajdy, Joseph and Leopold Toscana. In the end, we may find out that the new reinforcements of he tower are marked by dot lines, and that 800 men of the Turkish garrison were taken prisoners and that the booty consisted of 17 cannons and 20 (Turkish) flags. The upper right corner is marked by number 11.
