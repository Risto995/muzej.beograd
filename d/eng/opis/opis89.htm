ALBUM OF CUNO VON QUITZOW
PRINCE'S COURT AT BELGRADE

Representation of the so-called Stari konak - Old lodge (house of Stevan Simić), an object erected in 1840 and demolished after the royal couple - Aleksandar and Draga Obrenović, was assassinated in 1903. The house was purchased for the prince Aleksandar Karađorđević and adapted in line with his demands. The house looks, a fence encompassing palatial complex, guardians and passers clad in oriental clothes - a mixture of oriental, folk and European accouterments, are distinctly visible along with people entirely clad in European clothings. . Signature C. v. Quitzow 1856 is in the lower left corner.
