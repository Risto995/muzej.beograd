VIEW OF BELGRADE IN 1820

Representation of Belgrade viewed from Danube . The tones of the picture are very soft. From right to left are represented: a part of Belgrade fortress (downstream of Nebojša kula - tower) and Dorćol. In the foreground on Danube are the ships with Arabian sails whose appearance that does not correspond to Danubian boats. "Belgrade " is inscribed by tiny letters in the lower left corner, and G. Hering del. J.B. Pyne lith. in the right one. This sheet comes from Hering's work Sketches on the Danube in Hungary and Transylvania , published in London in 1838.
