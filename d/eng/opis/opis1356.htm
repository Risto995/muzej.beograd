Representation of Kapetan Mi{ino zdanje (edifice) at Studentski Trg no. 1, constructed in 1863 after the project of architect Jan Nevole , today the Rectorate of the University of Belgrade. The Russian chapel of general Tchernayev, hospital of the Russian Red Cross society and other institutions abided there during the Serbian-Turkish war. The shot was taken from Studentski Trg, so that main and lateral facade are seen, facing the new building of Faculty of Philosophy.
