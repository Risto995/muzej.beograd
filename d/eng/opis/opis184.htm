PLAN OF THE BELGRADE FORTRESS

New constructions of infantry and cavalry barracks and arsenal within the inner fortification (Castle) in the Upper town are denoted apart from the general plan of the fortress and new fortifications. Plan of the part of town is outside of the fortress. Plan of Zemun , identified as "a small place in Hungary") is placed at the "crest" of Sava. Separate map of the broader environs of Belgrade bordered by (Bački) Monoštor, Zvornik, Židovar ant the course of Resava. Scales for map and plan are marked separately. As for the plan 100 ells make 5,8 cm, i.e., the scale is cca 1 : 2.600, and for the map, the scale is expressed in French lieues.
