Representation of the interior of Old konak, demolished in 1903, most probably the interior of the so-called Red salon of prince Milan Obrenović (probably the central room at the floor) with military flags. According to a note at the back of picture, these are the flags of Russian volunteers (Eagles company, the Moscow-Tchernayev and Niznyenovgordian companies).

Author: Groman I. V.

Period: between 1876 and 1878

Place: Belgrade
