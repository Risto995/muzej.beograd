A view to the mouth of Sava. Lower part of Pariska St., and its turning into Karađođeva St. A long wooden fence is visible along with several houses. To the right is a road - the beginning of present Donjogradski bulevar, and in the background, across the river confluence is the silhouette of Zemun .
