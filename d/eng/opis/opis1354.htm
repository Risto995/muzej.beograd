Representation of the building of hotel "Srpska kruna" in Knez Mihajlova St. 56. It was constructed in 1870. The National Library was placed in it after the Second World War, and today (after a thorough reconstraction) it is the Library of the City of Belgrade. Its sides facing Knez Mihajlova and Pariska St. Are shown in the photograph.
