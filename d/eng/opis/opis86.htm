ALBUM OF CUNO VON QUITZOW
MILITARY ACADEMY AT BELGRADE

The building was located in Kneza Miloša street, at the corner with Birčaninova st. The architect Jan Nevole (1812-1903) designed it, and its original looks is here represented. The building was enlarged prior to 1878, and later it was rebuilt maintaining its original purpose. It was destroyed in German bombardment on 6 April 1941. Coloured picture represents the building surrounded by park and dense verdure, with several cadets in front of the main entrance.
