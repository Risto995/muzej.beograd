PANORAMA OF BELGRADE IN 1730-1739

Southern panorama of Belgrade, drawn by an Imperial engineer. The same text is represented in French (up left) and in German (below the panorama). Representation of Belgrade during Austrian reconstruction of the town and fortress. The fort (2), inner fortification - castle (3), new town fortifications (6), Serbian town on Sava (Savska varoš) below the fortress (8), German town (11), arsenal (gun-powder depot) on the Danube terrace (12), and three bastions (13, 14, and 15). Legend with 13 entries is in French and German language.

Author: Will. Johann Martin

Period: 1730-1739

Place: Augsbourg (?)

Object: engraving

Tehnique: copper engraving

Material: paper

Size of sheet: 31,4 x 21,2 cm

Legend: Text is in German and French language

Entries: panorama

Subentries: panorama of Belgrade
