BELGRADE, A VIEW FROM SAVA IN THE MID XIX CENTURY

Panorama represents Belgrade seen from southwest, with a number of new buildings. Among them, to the east of Saborna crkva (Cathedral church), the Old edifice is noted, demolished before the Second World War. Austrian watchtowers (karaula) are marked on the left bank of Sava and on Veliko ratno ostrvo (Great War island), a steamboat floating on Sava, and a silhouette of Zemun in the far left.
