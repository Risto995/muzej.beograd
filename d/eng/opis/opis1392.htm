Representation of the interior of Kapetan Mišino zdanje ( Kapetan Miša's edifice). The interior of Velika škola (High school) in Kapetan Mišino zdanje (Kapetan Miša's edifice - the present Rectorate of Belgrade University) is seen. One of the classrooms, "room 55", is modified into a chapel, for the needs of Russian volunteers and the staff of Russian Red Cross Society.

Period: between 1876 and 1878
