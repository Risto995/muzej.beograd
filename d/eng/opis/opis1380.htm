Representation of the hospital room with wounded men in Kapetan Miša's edifice ( Kapetan Mišino zdanje). Windows are lined along both sides (left and right), the entrance is in the room's bottom, and nurses and physician (?) are next to it. Six beds are visible, and orthopedic crutches are placed next to one of them.
