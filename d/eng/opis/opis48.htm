BODENEHR'S ALBUM (FIG. 28)
MAP OF BOSPHORUS WITH CONSTANTINOPLE (CONSTANTINOPEL)

Schematic plan of Constantinople divided to Galata and Pera, the town itself and Saray (palatial complex of "Seragli"), and a map of Bosphorus with all important sites marked at both of its coastlines. The upper left corner contains a cartouche with description and history of Constantinople, but the text, due to its large size, continues over and outside the cartouche to the right. Historical data are summarized since the foundation of town and Constantine the Great, until the Turkish conquest during Mohammed the Conqueror (El Fatih), although an inversion was made, and the year of Turkish victory is noted as 1543 instead of 1453.
