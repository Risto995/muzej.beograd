BATTLE SCENE (AND PANORAMA) OF BELGRADE

Representation of the sequence of events taking place since the beginning of the siege of Belgrade in September 1789 and during its occurrence. The crossing of Austrian army over Sava on 17 and 18 September is depicted, the arrangement of batteries (2) attacking the town, and among them a battery in the the foreground, on the left bank of Danube which supported the attack on the Lower town (3), and finally the marching echelon which penetrates the town across the palisades and continues to attack the fortress. The central part of picture is occupied by the panorama of Belgrade, viewed across Ratno ostrvo (the War island). The town is on fire and the struggle goes on with troops which entered the suburbia and the Lower town. The legend in French is placed below the engraving (to the left) and in German (to the right).
