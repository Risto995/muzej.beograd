The picture is dominated by triumphal gate made of logs, with large central archway and two smaller lateral ones. The upper part is all covered by foliage. Behind, to the left stands slightly damaged country house, and a cart with hay is on the road with group of people.

Note on the back: "Triumphal gate at Deligrad to welcome the Prince"
