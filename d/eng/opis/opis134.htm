PANORAMA OF BELGRADE WITH PLAN OF THE TOWN

Panorama of the town is described by 6 letter entries (A - F). It is an unsuccessful and unrealistic attempt to represent the town and new buildings constructed in the end of XVII century. The author presents a classic view of Belgrade viewed across the river (even with the hills in background), and the new fortification in foreground is placed in southern part of the town. A plan of Belgrade fortress is placed in the upper left corner, represented according to the siege of E. of Savoy troops in 1717 . The plan is in Italian language and it contains a legend with 9 entries (1-9). Tamiš (8) is erroneously noted as Tisa - "Tibisco".
