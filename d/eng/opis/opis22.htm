BODENEHR'S ALBUM (FIG. 2)
POLITICAL MAP OF HUNGARY AND NEIGHBOURING COUNTRIES

Hungary with neighbouring countries: Croatia, Dalmatia, Sclavonia, Transylvania , Moldavia, Wallachia, Bulgaria, and Rumelia (Romanien - the remaining Turkish possesions in Europe) as far as the Black Sea and Constantinople. Apart from specified countries, the map shows: Serbia and Bosnia, parts of Poland, Podolya, Macedonia. Frontiers are coloured, and a discontinuous line represents the frontiers after the treaty of Po`arevac.

Just in the upper right corner of the sheet is the number 101, a sign that some formerly created map was used in this album.

Period: after 1718

Place: Augsbourg
