PANORAMA OF BELGRADE IN THE MID XVII CENTURY

A panorama of Belgrade seen from the Danube with several ships floating on it. The legend with 8 letter entries contains the essential explanation. Among the noted objects there are: customs (F) in the Lower town harbour, in which the pasha's konak (lodge) (C) was erroneously marked as a castle, and Zemun (D) to the far right, marked as the village of Zeran
