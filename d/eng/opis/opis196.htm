PORTFOLIO OF PICTURES OF THE GREAT ĐORĐE PETROVIĆ KARAĐORĐE (FIG. 11)
REPRESENTATION OF THE BRAVERY OF STEVAN SINĐELIĆ WITH A VIEW OF ĆELE-KULA

Two pictures represented on a single sheet:

Left - A moment when Stevan Sinđelić , voivode of Resava, fires the gunpowder and destroys the trench with his soldiers and Turks who broke into the trench, during the battle of Čegar. Format of the left picture is 10 x 15,2 cm

Right - A view of Ćele-kula, constructed from the skulls of Sinđelić' combatants. Format of the right picture is 8 x 15,2 cm.

Legend: "Bravery of Stevan Sinđelić, voivode of Resava at kamenica on 19 May (1 June) 1809 with Ćele- kula.

Author: Nenadović Konstantin

Publisher: Printing firm "Schimek & Standart"

Period: 1883

Place: Vienna
