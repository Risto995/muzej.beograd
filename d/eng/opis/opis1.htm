ALBUM OF THE TREATY OF POŽAREVAC

Album of Jeremias Wolff dedicated to the treaty of Požarevac, settled on 21 July 1718 . The war of Habsburgs against the Turks in 1716- 1718 ended by this treaty. According to this treaty Austria has gained the following territories: the Sava valley, northern Serbia until Zapadna Morava, and Wallachia until Olt; Venice, its ally, gained a minor enlargement of its property in Dalmatia. The two decades long Austrian government in Serbia began by this treaty, which was terminated by defeats of Austria in 1737-1739 war. According to the treaty of Belgrade which followed it, the frontier between Austria and Turkey was fixed along the river courses of Sava and Danube . Apart from the introductory sheet, this album contains another 16 numerated and 2 non-numerated sheets, i.e., altogether 19 double sheets of the (approximate) format 74,5 x 30,5 cm.
