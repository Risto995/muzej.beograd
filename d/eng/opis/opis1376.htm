A view to the mouth of Sava. Courtyards and groundfloor houses in Karađorđeva St., are in the foreground. Three flags are visible, one of them to the far left is that of the red cross. Veliko ratno ostrvo (Great war island) is in the background and a silhouette of Zemun behind it.
