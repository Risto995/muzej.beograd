PORTFOLIO OF PICTURES OF THE GREAT ĐORĐE PETROVIĆ KARAĐORĐE (FIG. 22)
PORTRAIT OF KONSTANTIN N. NENADOVIĆ

Portrait of the author of the album, Konstantin N. Nenadović , an artillery captain in retirement. In contrast to other depictions placed each at a separate sheet, this one is printed at the back of sheet representing Karađorđe (FI1/5103). A man of mature age is portrayed, in officer's uniform, with mayor stars, leans by the left hand on official Serbian officer's sabers.

Legend: Konstantin N. Nenadović of the Royal Serbian artillery, captain in retirement
