SCENE FROM THE SIEGE OF BELGRADE IN 1789

Representation of the events which took place in Belgrade, on the bank of Danube , on 12 October 1789 when the defeated Turkish garrison departed Belgrade as it was anticipated by the agreement on capitulation. The row of Turkish soldiers and civilians with children is depicted on its way out of devastated Belgrade, through the opened town gate. Town walls are already adorned by Austrian banners. The siege commander, baron Laudon watches the embarkment of Turks and setting sail of the boats to Orsava and Vidin, accompanied by his escort. A short text in German language below the depiction explains that the siege of Belgrade lasted from 10 September till 8 October 1789 .
