ALBUM OF THE TREATY OF POŽAREVAC (FIG. 13)

In the front is a group of cavalrymen consisting of 15 ichoghlans (litsch agasi), obviously from aristocratic families, probably the boys because they are beardless (ohne Barte). Behind them are two high Court attendants, followed by a squadron of 15 cavalrymen, the servants in Saray - the Court. In the rear are two hippopotamuses (a gift -?) followed by 12 infantrymen armed with guns, the chaushs ("Czuhutarars").
