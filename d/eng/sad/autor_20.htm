Baeck Elias (1679 - 1747)
He was also called Heldenmuth - "Brave man". He studied in Rome, and lived and worked in Venice and Ljubljana (Laibach), and finally, in his birthplace, Augsbourg where he died. He painted and engraved various landscapes, portraits, battles... Some of his works are signed as follows: E. B. A. H., i.e., Ellias Baeck alias Heldenmuth. He is known after his work "Atlas geographicus" published in 1710 at Saxe-Weimar.
