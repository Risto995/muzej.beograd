Katana (tur. equestrian, soldier)
So-called "Katanska buna" (rebellion of Katanas) took place during the government of Ustavobranitelji /constitutionalists/ (1842-1859). Supporters of the cast out Obrenović family, clad into cavalry uniforms, attempted to invoke a mutiny of people.
