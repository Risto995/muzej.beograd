Živković Hadži-Nikola (Voden 1792 - Belgrade 1870)
Živković was the main master builder during the epoch of "Serbia of Knez Miloš ". There is no evidence about his education, nor about the date of his arrival to Serbia. His works began with design of the Konak of Princess Ljubica. During Miloš 's exile, Živković resided in Zemun .
