Savoy-Carignan, Eugen de (1663-1736)
Austrian army commander, born French, enlisted into the army of Leopold I. Participated in great Viennese war. Seriously wounded as the commander of regiment during the siege of Belgrade in 1688 . In the battle of Senta (1697) became the commander of Austrian army, and soon afterwards the chief commander in the war against Turkey (1716-18) as well as in the wars against France. He became famous in the battles of Senta (1697), Petrovaradin (1716) and Belgrade ( 1717 ).
