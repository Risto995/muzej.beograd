Edirne - Jedrene, Adrianopel, Hadrianopolis
Town in the nortwestern part of European Turkey. During Murat I (1359-1389), it became the capital of the Ottoman Empire (instead of Bursa). Since the conquest of Constantinople (1455) Edirne was a summer residence of the Sultans.
