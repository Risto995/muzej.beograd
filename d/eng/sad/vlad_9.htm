Sigismund (@igmund, @igmond, ruled 1368-1437)
The son of Charles IV of Luxembourg who gained the throne of Hungary-Croatia by marriage (1387), and after his father's death (1411) became German Emperor, and Czech King a few years later (1418). He organized a crusade against Turks, but was defeated at Nikopol in 1396. He established "Dragon order" of the knights, its member being also Despot Stefan Lazarevi} to whom he conferred the town of Belgrade.
