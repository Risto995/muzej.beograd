Delija - deli
(tur. deli - wild, crazy), otherwise a branch of Turkish cavalry established in the sanjak of Smederevo in XV century by Bali-bey Jahjapašić. They were recruited in the Balkans and wore hides of fierce animals and wings of predatory birds.
