Petrović, Hajduk-Veljko (around 1780 - Negotin entrenchment 1813)
One of the famous heroes of the First Insurrection. Hajduk-Veljko was in the unit of Stanoje Glavaš already in 1803, and participated in the insurrection since its very beginning. He fought many battles and became particularly famed after liberation of Crna Reka (1807) and defense of Soko Banja (1809). He obtained the title of voivode in 1811, and was killed by a cannon ball during defense of Negotin and Krajina.
