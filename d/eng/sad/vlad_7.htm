Mohammed II the Conqueror (ruled 1451-1481), the sultan
Along with Suleiman the Magnificent, he was the greatest Turkish ruler. He conquered Constantinople (1453), the Serbian Despotate (1459), but also established a number of laws working up the interior consolidation of Turkey.
