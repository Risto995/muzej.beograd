Ilić Vule, Kolarac (Kolare ? - Bucharest 1834)
When the insurrection started, Kolarac escaped from jail and joined the rebels. During the attack on Belgrade (1806) he bombarded the Lower town with two cannons placed on Veliko Ratno ostrvo. Afterwards he distinguished himself in the battle of Suvodol (near Sjenica 1809). Between 1810 and 1813 he was the commander of Smederevo , and defender of the Morava crossing at Kulič. After the downfall of insurrection, he abandoned Serbia and arrived to Russia (Hotin). Although he declared a wish to return, Miloš Obrenović did not let him. He died in Bucharest.
