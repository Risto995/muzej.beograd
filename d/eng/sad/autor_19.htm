Nyport, Justus van den (1625 - Utrecht 1692)
Dutch painter and graphic artist. On several occasions he sojourned in Central Europe, having painted a number of churches as well. Around 1686 he had illustrated the manual of geometry by A. F. Burchardt von Birckenstein, with a series of 110 views of various towns.
