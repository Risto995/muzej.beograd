Goebel Karl (1824-1899)
Viennese painter and cartographer, student of the lithographer J. Krihuber. Invited by prince Mihailo, he sojourned in Serbia in 1861, and later also, in 1881. During his stay in Belgrade he made several aquarelles, lithographs and a portrait of princess Natalija.
