Lazarević, Pop Luka (Svileuva 1774 - Šabac 1852)
A priest distinguished as courageous leader of the First Insurrection. According to his merits he became the voivode and commander of Šabac. Between 1813 and 1832 lived in emigration. After Aleksandar Karađorđević came to power, he was appointed as counselor.
