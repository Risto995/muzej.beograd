Lazarevi}, Pop Luka (Svileuva 1774 - [abac 1852)
A priest distinguished as courageous leader of the First Insurrection. According to his merits he became the voivode and commander of [abac. Between 1813 and 1832 lived in emigration. After Aleksandar Kara|or|evi} came to power, he was appointed as counselor.
