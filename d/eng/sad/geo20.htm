Savamala (turc. Mahala Sava - quarter along Sava)
Part of Kalemegdan- Terazije ridge descending toward Sava. In a narrow sense, it is the part of Karađorđeva St. with a network of small streets upstream of Svetonikoljski trg. The largest, central part of Savamala was demolished and rearranged during bridge construction (Bridge of the King Aleksandar - Tramway bridge).
