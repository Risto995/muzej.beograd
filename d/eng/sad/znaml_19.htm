Nenadović (Aleksin) Sima (Brankovina 1793 - Dublje 1815)
After death of his father, his uncle Jakov sent him to Srem to finish school at Kupinovo, then to Sremski Karlovci (where he finished two grades of Lycaeum) and Vinkovci where he finished the school for cadets in 1809. Subsequently he enlisted to Austrian army. He arrived to Serbia in 1813, where he was appointed for the voivode of Kolubara district. In autumn, after Serbian collapse, he escaped to Srem and enlisted to Austrian army as the volunteer in the war against Napoleon. He returned to Serbia in 1815, and was killed in the battle of Dublje leading his countrymen from Valjevo.
