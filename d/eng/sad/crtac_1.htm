Nessenthaler, Johann David (1695-1736)
No data about him, but Elias Nessenthaler is well known (1664-1714 Augsbourg ) draftsman and engraver who published engravings in illustrated calendars. It seems likely that our J. D. N. was his son or relative, and his successor in any event.
