Janke Franc (beginning of XIX century - ?)
Janke worked as an engineer in Serbia since 1834. Projects of several buildings constructed in Belgrade are ascribed to him: Đumrukana /customs/ (1835), house of Cvetko Rajić - Realka (1837), Saborna crkva /Cathedral church/ - on old foundation (1837-41). He worked on urbanist plans for new parts of Belgrade and other towns in Serbia.
