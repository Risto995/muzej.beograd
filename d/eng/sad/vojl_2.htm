Laudon Gideon (1717 -1790)
Scottish born low nobleman enlisted in the army of Habsbourg, having advanced to the rank of marshall. He distinguished himself in the war against Friedrich II (Prussian) and in the siege of Belgrade 1789 , in the last Austrian-Turkish war.
