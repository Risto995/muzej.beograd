Petrovi} \or|e, Kara|or|e (1768-1817)
After killing a Turk, he fled to Austria in 1787, and enlisted as volunteer to participate in the war(1788-91). On 2 February 1804 elected for the leader of insurrection . He distinguished himself by victories at Mi{ar and Deligrad in 1806, and liberated Belgrade in the same year. He formed the alliance with Russians and in 1809 started an offensive to Montenegro. However, after Turkish intrusion from Ni{, he abandoned the offensive. After the disastrous year of 1813, he abandoned Serbia, and later, from Austria, he arrived to Bessarabia. He joined "Hetaireia", a secret Greek society which intended to organize joint action of all Balkan peoples and finally drive the Turks away. He returned to Serbia on 27 August 1817 intending to persuade Milo{ Obrenovi} to fight against the Turks. But, Milo{ Obrenovi} ordered his assassination, and after decapitation, his head was stuffed with hay and sent to Constantinople. He is the progenitor of the dynasty of Kara|or|evi}.
