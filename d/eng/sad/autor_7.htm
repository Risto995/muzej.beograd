Ostertag Heinrich Jonas (active around 1711)
Copper engraver working at Augsbourg in 1711. He accomplished several good plans and views of the towns. He worked as illustrator in editions of Jeremias Wolff. A plan of Augsbourg in 1719 was made after Ostertag's drawing.
