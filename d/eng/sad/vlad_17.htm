Maximilian II Emmanuel of Bavaria (ruled 1679-1726)
Bavarian elected prince - by his title and rank member of the congregation which (formally) elects the Holy Roman Emperor. He distinguished himself in 1683- 1699 wars with Turkey, and especially on the occasions of conquest of Buda (1686) and Belgrade ( 1688 ) as the chief commander of imperial army.
