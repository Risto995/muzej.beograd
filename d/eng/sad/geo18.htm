Rumelija - Rumelia
Turkish name for all European parts of the Empire, which were originally divided in two eyalets (pašaluk, beylerbey 's land) - Rum (Europe) and Anatolia. During further elaboration of the network of eyalets ( Buda , Timisoara, Kanjiža) the term Rum (from Rumelia - Romai, i.e. the Greeks) became more closely related to the central and southern Bulgaria.
