Tug - Tugh
tur. Banner of horse's tail (or mane) signifying the rank of its holder. Its origin is in "old" Turkish homeland of Altai. Pasha could have one tugh, sanjakbey two, viziers three, and great viziers four or even five tughs. Tugh signifies the old Turkish banner.
