Sandžak - Liva, Sanjak
Organizational unit of Turkish Empire related to Turkish military- feudal system, in principle one sanjak provides the army with one regiment (ordia). In our country there were the following sanjaks: Vučitrn, Prizren, Kruševac (Aladža Hisar), Beograd, Zvornik, Požega, etc. In the course of time, due to Austrian occupation of Bosnia in 1878, the term "sanjak" has primarily been related to so-called Novopazarski sanjak, i.e., the area of old Herzegovina and Raška.
