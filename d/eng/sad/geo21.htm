Sedmogradska - Transilvanija, Transylvania, Hung. Erdely, Siebenburgen
Region in northwestern Romania, a large plain (cca 60.000 km2) between the Carpathians (Transylvanian Alps) and Bihor Mt. Traditionally, this area was considered as a part of Hungary (although the Hungarian population hardly reaches 30% ). After the battle of Mohacs, the Erdely (Transylvania) became the vassal principality of Turkey, and an object of conflict between Turks and Habsbourgs. During the great Viennese war, the Emperor Leopold I had forced the last Transylvanian prince Michael Apafy to admit the supreme power of Vienna. It became a part of Habsbourg monarchy in 1691, but maintaining its autonomy until 1868. After I World War it became a part of Romania. Romanian tradition views Transylvania as a part of Romania: Wallachia, Moldavia and Transylvania.
