Second Serbian Insurrection - 1815
After the collapse of First insurrection , the ensuing Turkish violence has caused further rebellions. One of the leaders, Miloš Obrenović, although opposing to direct conflict with Turks, stood up ahead the people and after several victories (Ljubić, Dublje) started the negotiations. This process was actually accomplished only in 1830, when Turkey agreed to guarantee the autonomy of the Principality of Serbia and acknowledge Miloš Obrenović as hereditary prince, under the pressure of international circumstances.
