Seutter Mathias (1678-1757) cartographer and publisher
Homann's student. In 1707 started his own cartography workshop in Augsbourg . Since 1728 he began to publish various atlases (Atlas novus, Atlas minor and Grosser Atlas). After his death, his son Albrecht (1722- 1762) and sons-in-law T. C. Lotter (who later established a workshop under his own name) and G. B. Probst continued the work.
