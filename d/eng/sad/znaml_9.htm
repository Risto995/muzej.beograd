Katić Janko (Rogača ? - Krnjić 1806)
One of the distinguished heroes of the First Insurrection. He was an interpreter on the meeting with Turks in Zemun on 11 May 1804, organized in attempt of reconciliation of dahis with riotous people. Killed during local conflict with Turks near the village of Krnjić.
