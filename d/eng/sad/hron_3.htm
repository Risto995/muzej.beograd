Treaty of Karlovci - 1699
The great, so-called "Viennese war" ended by the treaty of Sremski Karlovci in 1699. It was preceded by the great victory of Eugene of Savoy over the Turks in the battle of Senta in 1697. According to the treaty, the Habsbourgs have acquired the following territories: Hungary, Slavonia and Srem west of the line Sremska Mitrovica - Slankamen.
