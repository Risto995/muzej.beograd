Čarapić Vasa (Beli Potok? - Belgrade 1806)
Čarapić participated in Austrian army as the volunteer (1787-88), and district assembly elected him prince (knez) of the district of Grocka , as a distinguished man and hero. Even before the insurrection in 1804 he became renegade (haiduk). He distinguished himself in the battle of Karanovac in 1805, and died during assault on the Stambol gate in Belgrade on 11 December 1806. His death is represented in the aquarelle by Anastas Jovanović.
