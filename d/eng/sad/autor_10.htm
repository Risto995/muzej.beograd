Westerhout, Arnold von (Antwerp 1651 - Rome 1725)
Draftsman, engraver, and painter. Until 1675 Westerhout lived in Antwerp, and subsequently in Prague. Since 1688 he lived and worked in Rome, with a pause of four years (1688-92) when he sojourned and worked in Florence.
