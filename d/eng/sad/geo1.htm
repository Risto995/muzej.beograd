Augsburg - Augsbourg
Town in southeast Germany, on the Lech river, not far from Munich. It was an official residence of Roman-German Emperors. The so-called "Church treaty of Augsburg" marked an end of the cycle of religious wars in Germany. The town was a seat of German bankers, the Fuger family. It was a large center of culture and economy during XVI-XIX century, however its importance has decreased in the ensuing period.
