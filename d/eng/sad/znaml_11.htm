Loma Arsenije (Dragolj ? - 1815)
Joined the insurrection with Milan Obrenović. Became voivode of Kačar in 1811 - and was frequently recognized under this title. In battles which preceded a downfall of insurrection he fought at Deligrad, and later surrendered to the Turks. Participated in the Second Insurrection since its beginning and died in a conflict with Turks during the siege of Rudnik.
