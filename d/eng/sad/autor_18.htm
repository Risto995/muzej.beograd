Berge, Pieter van den, (before 1680 - 1737)
Son of the publisher of the same name from Amsterdam, thus often confounded with him. Worked in Hamburg and Amsterdam as engraver. He was printer and publisher as well. He is often confounded with cartographer Peter van der Berg (alias Petrus Montanus) because of his name.
