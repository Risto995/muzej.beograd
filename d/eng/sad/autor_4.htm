Wittgens Julia
Less well known painter from Vienna. She painted the National assembly at Kragujevac which took place on the Day of Holy Transfiguration in 1861 (Preobra`enska skup{tina). Subsequently she had requested financial support to make a colour lithograph of that work but she was refused. However, it seems that she succeeded to find the support for accomplishment of the enterprise, because her works have been sold in various bookshops in the end of XIX century, under the title: Assembly on the Day of Holy Transfiguration, the Annunciation in Belgrade, Belgrade, etc.
