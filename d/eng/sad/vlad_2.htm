Murat I (ruled 1359-1389), the sultan
Transferred the Turkish capital into Edirne (Adrianople) . Defeated the brothers Mrnjavčević on Maritsa (near Černomen, the site of "Srpski grob" - Serbian grave) in 1371, and conquered the large parts of Bulgaria, Byzantine empire and Macedonia. He died in the battle of Kosovo in June 1389, during warfare with prince Lazar Hrebeljanović. However, his son Bayazid I (Yilderim - Thunderbolt) accomplished the battle victoriously.
