Glava{ Stanoje, Stamatovi} (? - 1815)
Glava{ became a renegade before insurrection in 1804 . Veljko Petrovi} (Hajduk Veljko) was also in his troop. In a gathering before insurrection Glava{ refused to be a leader. He distinguished himself in the battle of Deligrad in 1806. After the collapse of insurrection in 1813, he surrendered and Turks have absolved him, having appointed him to guard the road to Constantinople. He was killed during an attempt of arrestation after the rebellion of Had`i Prodan . His head was impaled in front of Pasha's konak at Belgrade.
