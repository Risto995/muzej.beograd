Nenadović Milivoj (Valjevo - 1824 - ?)
At an early age, at school, his gift for calligraphy was noted. After he had sold his estate in the end of 1841, he went to Vienna in order to study painting privately. He applied for scholarship a number of times, but without success. In 1846 he had returned to Belgrade and applied again for scholarship, but he was refused as non-talented, and there is no record about him since. His lithograph "Beograd u Srbiji" /Belgrade in Serbia/ was published as a supplement to the third edition of "Dejanija k istoriji srbskoga naroda" /Contributions to the history of Serbian people/, by Dimitrije Davidović.
