Ortelius Hieronymus (XVI-XVII century)
Well known German historian, Protestant expelled from Augsbourg . His work Chronologia oder historische Beschreibung..., printed in Nuremberg in 1602, was illustrated by Nuremberg' painter and engraver Johann Siebmacher (died in 1611, well known illustrator of books, author of maps, views of the towns and battle scenes, especially those fro European - Turkish wars. It also is noteworthy that his successors had sold his engraved plates, which were hence used in later times by other publishers, in various other works.
