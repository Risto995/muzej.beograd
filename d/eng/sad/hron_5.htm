Treaty of Požarevac - 1718
Treaty between Austria and Republic of Venice at one side and Turkey on the other. According to the treaty Austria obtained Banat, eastern Srem, Bosnian Sava valley and northern Serbia. At the same time Austria gained important trade favours for the territory of Turkey. The emperor Charles VI supported the establishment of (Austrian) Oriental company.
