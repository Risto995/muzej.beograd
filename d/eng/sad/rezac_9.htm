Weigel Johann Cristoph (? - 1726)
Known under the nick-name "Younger". After his brother Cristoph Weigel's death (1654-1726) he continued the large family enterprise of printing and publishing. His most important works were three portable atlases: that of Germany, World and Atlas of Astronomy. The enterprise was later continued by the firm Schneider-Weigel.
